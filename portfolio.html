Lesson Designer (Servant Interactive; Columbia, SC)

I was the lead developer on a curriculum development tool while employed by Servant Interactive called the Lesson Designer (LD). The LD is a web application that allows writers, editors, and designers to create lessons for the Servant Platform (codesk). Some of the functionality available to users of the LD:

  • Add narration (via iSpeech API) to a lesson's content
  • Insert various activities into a lesson such as guided tours of images, flashcards, word searches, timelines, click to reveal boxes, tracing, and images with labels that can be dragged or clicked to reveal.
  • Ability to publish thousands of lessons a time to the iPad application and web application for viewing by end users.
  • Lessons are packaged up for offline use on publish. This includes videos, images, fonts, audio clips, audio narration, and all activities.
  • On publish a lesson goes the a "Media Optimization Process" to put all assets on a CDN and optimize the media to help lessons load as fast as possible.
  • Ability to clone a lesson from one content provider to another.

Bluegill (Reachsmart Interactive; Columbia, SC)

Bluegill is a web application for managing and reporting on incentive-based advertising promotions. That is - ads that have a path users follow to get to various ads. At that point purchasing available products gives the user points that qualify him or her for different rewards.

Bluegill allowed members of the business development team to control many aspects of the "path". This includes the available input fields on the registration form, the order of the path (landing page -> registration page -> ad 1 -> ad 2 -> ads for points), and the location of ads on the ads for points page (the CPA wall). The location of ads had both manual and auto placement. The auto placement would order the ads based on performance.

As would be expected, there was also extensive reporting. Available metrics included clicks, impressions, gross revenue, net margin, and their derivatives. Metrics are viewed in aggragate by published and can be drilled down to see how each specific ad is performing. All metrics can be filtered by gender and date.

Webneers & EFNEP (Clemson University's Youth Learning Institute; Pickens, SC)
